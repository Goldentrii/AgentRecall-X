---
name: Ask before proceeding when ambiguous
description: When instructions are unclear, ask first
type: feedback
---

When the user's intent is ambiguous, ask a clarifying question before executing. A 5-second question beats 30 minutes of wrong work.

**Why:** User communicates in scattered bursts. Agent often misinterprets priority or scope.
**How to apply:** Gather all points, structurize, confirm before executing.
